Ingredients of Healthy Breakfast Cookies

  1. It's of first, mix together..
  2. You need 1 cup of Organic Blue Agave Nectar.
  3. It's 1 cup of Brown Sugar.
  4. It's 1 cup of Butter, softened.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of Unsweetened Applesauce.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of Baking Soda.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of Ground Cinnamon.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of Vanilla Extract.
  9. It's 1 tsp of Baking Powder.
  10. You need 1 tsp of Sea Salt, finely ground.
  11. It's 4 of Eggs, large.
  12. It's 6 1/2 cup of Six-grain cereal (like old-fashioned Oats) uncooked.
  13. Prepare 2 1/4 cup of Flour, all-purpose or Whole Wheat.
  14. It's 2 cup of raisins, nuts or dark chocolate chips.
  15. You need 1/2 cup of flaxseed meal, optional.

Healthy Breakfast Cookies step by step

  1. Mix first nine ingredients together well.
  2. Add in eggs, one at a time, until mixed in well..
  3. Add in Six-grain cereal, 2 cups at a time and mix well..
  4. mix in flour and any optional ingredients and finish mixing.
  5. cover and refrigerate for 10 - 20 minutes..
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F..
  7. Place Parchment onto a cookie sheet (or lightly spray with Olive oil).
  8. Drop by spoonful onto cookie sheet, spaced apart by about 1/2 inch or so and lightly mashed down..
  9. Bake for 9 - 11 minutes, until golden brown. Makes about 5 dozen cookies..
