Ingredients of Healthy Mixed Fruits Smoothie

  4. It's 1/2 cup of Half/Half Milk.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of Sugar (optional).
  6. It's 1 tbsp of Vanilla Extract (optional).

Healthy Mixed Fruits Smoothie instructions

  1. Mix frozen yogurt, juice, and milk.
  2. Make sure consistency of mixed product is somewhat thick.
  3. Add desired amount of fruit, save some pieces of fruit for decoration if wanted..
  4. Add in sugar and vanilla extract for added taste.
  5. Blend, and enjoy! :).
