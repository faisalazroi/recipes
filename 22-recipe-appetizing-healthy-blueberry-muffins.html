Ingredients of Healthy Blueberry Muffins

  1. It's 2 1/2 cups of flour.
  2. You need 1 tsp of baking soda.
  3. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of honey.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of brown sugar, loosely packed.
  7. You need 1 cup of mashed very ripe banana.
  8. It's 1/4 cup of plain yogurt.
  9. Prepare 1 of large egg, beaten.
  10. You need 3/4 cup of milk.
  11. It's 1 1/4 cup of blueberries.

Healthy Blueberry Muffins step by step

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Spray muffin tin with nonstick spray..
  2. Gently whisk flour, soda, salt and cinnamon in large bowl..
  3. In another bowl, mix together honey and brown sugar. Get out as many lumps as possible. Stir in banana, yogurt and egg..
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix together. Stir in milk slowly. Gently fold in blueberries..
  5. Fill muffin tin 1/2-3/4 way full with mixture..
  6. Bake for 17-18 minutes or until very light brown on edges. Cool 5 minutes before moving to wire rack to cool completely. Makes 12 muffins..
