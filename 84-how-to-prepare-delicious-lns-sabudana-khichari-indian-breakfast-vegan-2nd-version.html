L.N.S. SABUDANA KHICHARI INDIAN BREAKFAST (VEGAN) . 2nd version. step by step

  1. Take pre - soaked ,darined ( 2 hrs) SAGO SEEDS and above said ingredients..
  2. Take a khadhai put 2 tbsp of OIL in it. Put THYME, FENNEL SEEDS / ANISEEDS, CUMMINS SEEDS, AND GREEN CHILLIES (FINALLY CHOPPED) .Keeping flame at medium high , Now add chopped TOMATO and stir fry it for 3 minutes..
  3. Add SAGO SEEDS to the mixture stir and fry, it for 5 minutes..
  4. Cover the khadahi for 3 minutes. Keep the flame low medium..
  5. Again stir it for 5 minutes and and then again put the lid on it..
  6. Open the lid and Transfer the preparation into the waiting plate..
  7. Garnish it with FENNEL SEEDS / ANI SEEDS ..
