Ingredients of Mushroom n pepper omelette

  1. Prepare 6 of mushrooms.
  2. It's 1/2 of red pepper.
  3. You need 3 of eggs.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of woscestershire sauce.
  5. Prepare 6 tbsp of milk.
  6. You need 1 of salt n pepper.
  7. Prepare 1 pinch of mixed herbs.
  8. It's 1 pinch of chilli flakes.
  9. Prepare 2 of knobs of butter.

Mushroom n pepper omelette step by step

  1. Heat 2 knobs of butter in frying pan.
  2. dice the pepper and mushroom then fry for 1 minute then add a splash of woscestershire sauce.
  3. Mix eggs and milk in with the herbs, chilli flakes, salt and pepper.
  4. Making sure you use a non stick pan!! pour egg mixture over mushrooms and peppers.
  5. Let cook for 2 minutes then fold in half. Cook on both sides for half a minute each. And theres your tasty omelette :) enjoy..
