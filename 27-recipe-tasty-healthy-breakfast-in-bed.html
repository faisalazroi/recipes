Healthy breakfast in bed step by step

  1. Heat up some butter in a nonstick pan (i prefer to use Happy Call), add in the instant baked beans, close the lid for about 2 mins.
  2. Add in 2 tablespoon of tomato sauce, close the lid and let it cook.
  3. Shift the baked beans to one side of the pan, crack the egg and close the lid for about 2 mins.
  4. Add in a pinch of white pepper and salt, then your dish is ready to serve.
